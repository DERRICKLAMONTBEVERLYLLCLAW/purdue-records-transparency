Purdue Records Transparency

by Derrick LaMont Beverly

Purpose: To expose, archive, and advocate for public access to records, investigations, and unresolved student matters involving Purdue University.

This project repository contains:

  • Public correspondence with Purdue IT and administration
  • Student testimony and FOIA-style requests
  • Formal complaint filings and open records summaries
